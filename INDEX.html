About Me

Hello, I'm IGBOAYAKA FRANCIS MARYANTHONY. I am a passionate frontend developer with a keen eye for detail and a love for creating visually stunning and user-friendly websites.

I graduated with a degree in Computer Science from FLIP-TO-TECH and have been honing my skills in web development ever since.

My ultimate goal is to continue pushing the boundaries of what's possible in web development and to make a positive impact on the digital world.
